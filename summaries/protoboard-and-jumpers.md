### What is Protoboard?
A breadboard (or protoboard) is a reusable prototyping board that allows you to build electrical circuits without needing to solder. It allows you to quickly insert components and jumper wires.

Most of these circuits are built in the center terminal strips, where we have columns numbered 1 to 30. Each column has 5 interconnected points, but they have no connection to the adjacent columns.

The outer power rails are where you power the circuit. In Arduino projects, you usually connect 5V to the red rail (+) and GND to the blue or black rail (-).

### What is a Jumper?

A jumper (or jumper wire) is an electrical wire used to connect points on a PCB or a breadboard.
